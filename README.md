Hello! Are you a beginner or someone who wants to strengthen collaboration skills? This repo will guide you step by step.

For absolute beginners, we have tutorials and then quick tasks to try out for each and every basic concept of git.
Based on your ease and expereince explore and learn as you wish.
If you already experienced feel free to contribute to this repository and share your learnings/resources with everyone.

# How This Repo Works
Each module contains:
📘 tutorial.md → Learn the concept
🧪 task.md → Practice it hands-on
